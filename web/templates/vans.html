Vans

Save shared vans here, then assign them to drivers during Event Planning when personal vehicles are not enough.

How Vans Work

1
Save the van here once.

Add each van with the number of passenger seats it provides.

2
Select drivers on Event Planning.

After you pick drivers, a Van Assignments section appears below the driver list.

3
Assign one van to one driver for that event.

That driver keeps their route, but the van's seat count replaces their personal vehicle capacity for that event only.

Saved Vans

Vans are optional overflow capacity. Each van can be assigned to one driver per event.
